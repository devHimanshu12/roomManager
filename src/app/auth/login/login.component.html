Welcome to RoomManager.
Simplify your meeting room bookings with just a few clicks!
Follow these steps to book meeting room.
  • Login using any user name and password sent on mail.
  • After successful login you will routed dashboard and will have overview of the bookings.
  • Click on Book Meeting and fill all the details.
  • After booking meeting rooms, upcomong and room details are available in table format in dashboard.
  • You can also check the status of rooms by click on Book meeting button and then click check Status button.
